Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 30, 2025

The Short Version: We do not collect any data. Period. My Heart Log has no servers, no analytics, and no way to access your information. Your data stays entirely on your device and in your personal iCloud account.

Data Collection

My Heart Log does not collect any data from you. We have no servers, no databases, and no means of receiving information from the app. Everything you enter stays on your device.

Where Your Data Lives

All data you enter into My Heart Log is stored:

  • On your device: Your journal entries are stored locally on your iPhone or iPad using Apple's secure data storage.
  • In your iCloud (optional): If you enable iCloud sync, your data is stored in your personal iCloud account. This sync is handled entirely by Apple's CloudKit technology. We never see or access this data.

iCloud Sync

When you enable iCloud sync:

  • Your data syncs directly between your devices through your personal iCloud account
  • Apple manages this sync using CloudKit
  • We have no access to your iCloud data
  • You can disable sync at any time in the app settings

For information about how Apple handles iCloud data, please see Apple's Privacy Policy.

Notifications

If you enable reminders, the app uses Apple's local notification system. These notifications are scheduled and managed entirely on your device. No notification data is sent to us or any third party.

No Third-Party Services

My Heart Log does not connect to any external services. There are no analytics, no advertising, and no tracking of any kind.

Data Export

You can export your data as a PDF report at any time through the app. This export is generated locally on your device and is only shared where you choose to send it.

Children's Privacy

My Heart Log is not directed at children under 13. As stated above, we do not collect any data from any users, including children.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, we will post the new version here with an updated date. Significant changes will be communicated through the app.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please visit our support page for contact information.

Medical Disclaimer

My Heart Log is a personal journal application for keeping track of your own records. It is not a medical device and is not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any disease. Always consult with qualified healthcare providers for medical advice.
